About Me

I help organizations strengthen their teams, systems, and programs to build the strategic alignment needed to achieve greater and more measurable impact. With over 10 years of experience supporting nonprofits, Indigenous Nations, public-sector agencies, and global institutions such as the International Finance Corporation of the World Bank Group, I bring both strategic clarity and relational depth to each engagement.

My background includes small and medium enterprise development, policy reform, workforce development, Indigenous economic development, and reconciliation policy. As a Certified Project Management Professional with a Master’s in Public Policy, I specialize in inclusive program design, partnership development, measurement and evaluation, and relationship‑centred engagement grounded in humility and accountability.
